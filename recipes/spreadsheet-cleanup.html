Field Walkthrough 06 Spreadsheet Cleanup and Anomaly Review

This task did not start by asking for a result. It first checked whether the scene was specific enough: A redacted order CSV had blank statuses, abnormal amounts, and duplicates needing reviewable cleanup suggestions.
