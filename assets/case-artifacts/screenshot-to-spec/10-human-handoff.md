# 07 设计截图转实现规格 - 人工交接清单

## 中文

- 截图不能证明交互状态，缺失状态必须标为待确认。
- 规格只写可实现信息，不臆测业务规则。
- 实现后必须用同一视口重新截图对比。

## English

- Screenshots cannot prove interaction states, so missing states must be marked for confirmation.
- The spec records implementable details, not guessed business rules.
- After implementation, recapture the same viewports for comparison.
