# 01 演示稿生成与导出核查 - 人工交接清单

## 中文

- 演示稿可用于内部评审，不能直接对外发布。
- 用户需要确认试用期、隐私表述和素材授权。
- 若要改成正式销售材料，先补真实数据和品牌终审。

## English

- The deck is ready for internal review, not direct external publication.
- The user must confirm trial wording, privacy wording, and asset rights.
- To turn it into sales material, add real data and brand approval first.
