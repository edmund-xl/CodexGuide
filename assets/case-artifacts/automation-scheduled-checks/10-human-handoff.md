# 12 自动化提醒与定期检查 - 人工交接清单

## 中文

- 自动化不能替代需要人工判断的动作。
- 没有退出条件的定期任务不要长期保留。
- 失败提醒应说明证据和下一步，而不是只报错。

## English

- Automation cannot replace actions requiring human judgment.
- Do not keep scheduled tasks without an exit condition indefinitely.
- Failure alerts should include evidence and next action, not just an error.
